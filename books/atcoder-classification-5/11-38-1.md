$$
distance(\boldsymbol{p},\boldsymbol{q}) = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n(p_i - q_i)^2}
$$

$$
\sqrt{(x_1 - x_2)^2 + (y_1 - y_2)^2}
$$
