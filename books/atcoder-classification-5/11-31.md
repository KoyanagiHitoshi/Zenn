* [外積](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E3%82%AF%E3%83%AD%E3%82%B9%E7%A9%8D)

$$
\boldsymbol{u} =
\begin{pmatrix}
u_1 \\
u_2 \\
u_3 \\
\end{pmatrix}
,
\boldsymbol{v} =
\begin{pmatrix}
v_1 \\
v_2 \\
v_3 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol{u} × \boldsymbol{v}=
\begin{pmatrix}
u_2 v_3 - u_3 v_2 \\
u_3 v_1 - u_1 v_3 \\
u_1 v_2 - u_2 v_1 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

```python:サンプルコード
u = [1, 2, 3]
v = [4, 5, 6]
print(u[1]*v[2]-u[2]*v[1], u[2]*v[0]-u[0]*v[2], u[0]*v[1]-u[1]*v[0])
```

```text:実行結果
-3 6 -3
```

```python:サンプルコード
import numpy
u = [1, 2, 3]
v = [4, 5, 6]
print(numpy.cross(u, v))
```

```text:実行結果
[-3  6 -3]
```
