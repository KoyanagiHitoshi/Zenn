* [転置行列](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%BB%A2%E7%BD%AE%E8%A1%8C%E5%88%97)

$$
A =
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
A^T =
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 4 & 7 \\
2 & 5 & 8 \\
3 & 6 & 9 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

```python:サンプルコード
matrix = [[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6], [7, 8, 9]]
transpose = list(zip(*matrix))
print(transpose)
```

```text:実行結果
[(1, 4, 7), (2, 5, 8), (3, 6, 9)]
```

```python:サンプルコード
matrix = [[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6], [7, 8, 9]]
transpose = list(map(list, zip(*matrix)))
print(transpose)
```

```text:実行結果
[[1, 4, 7], [2, 5, 8], [3, 6, 9]]
```
