---
title: "　　12.7.3 列集計"
---

```text:入力
###
.#.
.##
```

```python:サンプルコード
grid = [input() for _ in range(3)]
print(*[col.count("#") for col in zip(*grid)])
```

```text:実行結果
1 3 2
```
